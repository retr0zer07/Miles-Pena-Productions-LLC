About Miles Pena

Miles Pena is a singer and recording artist with deep Latin roots, building a genuine connection with audiences since 2007 through music that speaks from the heart.

The Story

Miles Pena began his musical journey in 2007 with a simple conviction: music should feel real. Over the years, he has crafted songs that blend the emotional depth of Latin music with honest storytelling, earning the loyalty of a growing fan base across the United States and beyond.

His voice carries experience, passion, and a profound respect for the culture that shaped him. Whether performing live or releasing new material, Miles stays committed to music that connects — not just sounds good.
